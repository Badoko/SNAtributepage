Sule Nma Abdullahi

The man who lived by example

Here's a time line of Sule Nma Abdullahi's life:

  • 1960 - Born in Bida, Niger State
  • 1966 - Attended his first school at St. john missionary school bida
  • 1970 - Moved from bida to Zaria, where he began his Secondary school education
  • 1978 - Began a Bsc Econs degree program and Ahmadu Bello Universty, Zaria
  • 1982 - Got married to Fatima Nmakpan at Bida
  • 1984 - Got his first Son Aminu Sule Nma Abdullahi
  • 1988 - Completed an Msc degree program at Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria
  • 1990 - Founded Aquarius Softwares Nigeria, a leading software development company in the country
  • 2005 - Moved back to Niger State
  • 2020 - Died at his resident in Bida, Niger State at the age of 60
"Sule's life and achievement are testimony to the far-reaching contribution that one man's towering intellect, persistence and scientific vision can make to human peace and progress."
